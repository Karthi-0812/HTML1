SK College of Engineering (SKCE)

About SKCE

SK College of Engineering (SKCE) is one of the premier engineering institutions in Tamil Nadu. The college is known for its academic excellence, research activities, and placement opportunities.

Departments Offered

  1. Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
  2. Information Technology (IT)
  3. Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
  4. Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)
  5. Mechanical Engineering
  6. Civil Engineering

Campus Facilities

  • Central Library
  • Computer Laboratories
  • Hostel Facilities
  • Sports Complex
  • Wi-Fi Enabled Campus
  • Placement and Training Cell
